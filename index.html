FAQ

Common questions.

Does it work with my current phone number?
Yes. You keep your existing number. You simply set up call forwarding to our system — a one-time 2-minute setup from your phone carrier's settings. No new hardware, no new SIM card.
What if the AI makes a mistake on an order?
The AI reads back every order to the customer before confirming. Customers can correct any item before hanging up. You'll also see every order on your dashboard before preparing it, giving you a second chance to catch anything unusual.
How does the kitchen dashboard work?
Orders appear on a web-based tablet dashboard in real time. Any device with a browser works — iPad, Android tablet, or even a laptop. No app to install. Orders show the full items, customer name, callback number, and pickup or delivery details.
What languages does it support?
Currently English, Spanish, and Arabic. The AI detects the caller's language and responds accordingly. Additional languages available on request.
Can it answer more than one call at the same time?
Yes — unlike a single staff member, OrderTaker can answer unlimited calls simultaneously. If five customers call during your Friday dinner rush, all five get answered instantly and have their orders taken in parallel. No busy signals, ever.
What happens during business hours vs. after hours?
You set your hours during onboarding. During open hours, the AI takes live orders for immediate prep. After closing, OrderTaker stays on — it tells callers you're currently closed, shares your hours, answers menu and location questions, and can take a pre-order for your next opening time. Pre-orders land on your dashboard marked "Scheduled" so your team sees them first thing when you open.
I have more than one restaurant. How does pricing work?
Your first location is $199/month. Every additional location gets 15% off — $169.15/month each — billed together on one account so you can manage all your restaurants' orders and dashboards in one place.
Does OrderTaker integrate with my POS system (Toast, Square, Clover)?
OrderTaker works with any restaurant out of the box — no POS integration is required. Orders appear on a dedicated tablet dashboard, formatted for your kitchen, so staff can read and prep immediately. Direct POS integrations (starting with Toast and Square) are in development and will roll out to all customers at no extra charge as they become available.
Does the AI try to upsell customers?
Yes — the AI can suggest relevant add-ons — drinks, appetizers, sides, or desserts — based on what's ordered, using prompts you approve during setup. This is optional and fully customizable to match your menu and tone.
What if a customer has a complaint or unusual request?
OrderTaker is built for the routine volume — orders, reservations, hours, and menu questions. If a caller has a complaint, a complex custom request, or asks for a manager, the AI politely takes their name and number and flags it for your team to call back, rather than guessing. You can also set up live transfer to a manager's line during business hours.
Is there a contract or setup fee?
No contract, no setup fee. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. Onboarding and training is included in your first month.
